I’m 27 years old. I studied Fine Arts in Viana do Castelo and later Design in Barcelos.

As a kid, I used to watch anime like Naruto, One Piece, Bleach, Death Note...

Later on, I got into Evangelion, Cowboy Bebop, Vinland Saga, HxH, Mob Psycho...

Then I started reading manga like Berserk, Gantz, JJK, Chainsaw Man, Monster...

My main references are Berserk, Gantz, Vinland Saga, and Vagabond.

From these influences, I’m creating original stories that

merge those narrative styles within a Portuguese context.

By publishing independently, I keep full creative control — but it also means it takes me longer to get things done lol.
